Honey Glazed Chicken

This is what the person who submitted the recipe to AllRecipes says about it: "This honey chicken dish is easy to make with just 5 ingredients. I've used this recipe for years, and my family requests it often. Delicious served on steamed rice and garnished with sliced green onions."

This is one of the most straightforward recipes out there as the ingredient list is super short and the steps are very easy to follow. If you like it give it a go! I hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup honey
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk honey, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes in a bowl; set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir chicken in hot oil until lightly brown, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour honey mixture into the skillet; continue to cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and sauce is thickened, about 5 minutes more.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
